Welcome to Gurus of Comedy.  It is the premiere podcast on comedy to be found on this little thing called the internet.

It’s inception was born out of the part of a night of stand-up comedy that the audience doesn’t see.  When the performing comics get together after the gig to have a chat.  No one knows comedy quite like comedians, it is like watching precision watchmakers taking apart an iphone. This podcast brings to you what that experience is like. 

So if it is comedy and it exists on this small planet we’ll cover it. Interviews, books, movies, you name it, we cover it.

Thanks for joining us, with your presence you are truly spoiling us!
